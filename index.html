The Core Thesis
Speed figures measure how fast a horse actually ran. When the fastest horse on paper is going off at 3/1+ it means the crowd is betting something else — trainer hype, recency bias, jockey popularity. The system bets that the crowd is wrong more often than it's right. A horse at 5/1 should win ~17% of the time in a fair market. Our system hits 33%+ across all qualifying bets. That's the edge.
